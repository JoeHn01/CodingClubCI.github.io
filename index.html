About us

The College of Idaho Coding Club is an organization of alumni and students The College of Idaho. Our goal is to help students improve their programming skills to make sure that they are ready to join the workforce when the time comes. Our github page can be found here. This website is entirely maintained by members of the Coding Club that have an interest in web development.
